Scale Lab

Web Design, 2017

ScaleLab provides a way to to optimize influencers’ content and expand their audience to maximize impact on platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, and Twitter. During the initial release, brands were having trouble running campaigns and sponsoring influencer posts. My job was to find out why, and build a simple user experience to organize campaigns, track analytics, and manage budgets.

To make a great product, I had to know our user. I sat down with the team and led the persona research. Once we had a clear idea of our ideal user, I mapped out the general flow of campaign creation, and sketched out some analytics features. The key was to build simple flows, prototype them in Invision, and make improvements.
