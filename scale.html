Beyond US

Branding & Product Design, 2012

Beyond US is a social clothing company created with the objective to raise awareness and funding for local charities through unique t-shirts. The U.S. based brand donates a portion of profits to various non-profit partners in the United States. My role within this company was to define the brand through graphic, logo, and product design— giving the line a distinct style, while capturing the company's significance and mission.

When I hit the drawing board to brainstorm the initial t-shirt design, I faced a real challenge— to create a minimal, subtle design, yet a powerful conversation starter. I thought of a trick that could potentially provide that unique factor we were searching for. If I designed the t-shirt asymmetrically, it would look edgy and unusual—but could definitely serve as a conversation starter! *Coincidentally, that same day I happened to walk by a local blood drive, and noticed everyone wearing bright, colorful ribbons around their left arms. It was perfect! It was subtle, yet bright and powerful, and best of all, it let others know that you had donated and helped others in need.

I started the logo by jotting down the most significant aspects of the company. I used the colors red, white, and blue because the company was based in the U.S., and focused solely on giving back to U.S. based charities. The three stripes would resemble the colorful sleeve designs I had given the t-shirts. The company turned out to be an absolute success, and I feel fortunate to have been a part of it to drive sales, and ultimately donations.
