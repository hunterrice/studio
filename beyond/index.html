Beyond US

Branding & Product Design, 2012

Beyond US is a social clothing company I created with the objective to raise awareness and funding for local charities through unique t-shirts. The U.S. based brand donates a portion of profits to various non-profit partners in the United States. My role within this company was to define the brand through graphic, logo, and product design— giving the line a distinct style, while capturing the company's significance and mission.

When I hit the drawing board to brainstorm the initial t-shirt design, I faced a real challenge— to create a minimal, subtle design, yet a powerful conversation starter. I thought if I could pull off a unique, asymmetric design, it could be edgy and unusual enough to attract attention.



The idea initially struck me when I walked by a local blood drive, and noticed everyone wearing bright, colorful ribbons around their left arms. It was perfect! It was subtle, yet bright and powerful, and best of all, it let others know that you had donated and helped others in need.

Below are some of the initial sleeve pattern mockups.

I started on the logo by jotting down the most significant aspects of the company. I used the colors red, white, and blue because the company was based in the U.S., and focused solely on giving back to U.S. based charities. The three stripes would resemble the colorful sleeve designs I had given the t-shirts.
To put it all together, I strategized a social media marketing campaign for customers to share their experiences wearing the t-shirt. The theme was "ADVENTURE BEYOND", and encouraged people to take incredible photos around the world, while using #adventurebeyond. Enjoy some of my favorites I found!
